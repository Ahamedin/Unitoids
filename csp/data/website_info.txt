## About Us
Freelancer Connect is a leading online platform connecting businesses with skilled freelancers worldwide. Our mission is to make hiring top talent fast, safe, and transparent. We offer a wide range of services including web development, graphic design, content writing, mobile app development, SEO, marketing, and more.

## Services Offered
- Web Development: Build websites, e-commerce platforms, and web applications.
- Graphic Design: Logos, branding, marketing materials, and UI/UX design.
- Mobile App Development: iOS, Android, cross-platform apps.
- Content Writing: Blogs, articles, marketing copy, technical writing.
- SEO & Marketing: Search engine optimization, social media marketing, ad campaigns.
- Other Freelance Services: Translation, video editing, consulting, and more.

## How It Works
1. Post a Project: Create a job posting detailing your requirements.
2. Browse Freelancers: Search freelancers by skill, rating, and location.
3. Hire a Freelancer: Select the best candidate based on skills, rating, and project history.
4. Payment & Milestones: Pay securely through our platform using milestones.
5. Review & Feedback: Provide feedback and ratings to maintain quality.

## FAQs
Q: How do I find the best freelancer?
A: Use our search filters for category, rating, city, and completed projects. Check freelancer profiles and reviews.

Q: How do I pay a freelancer?
A: Payments are processed securely through the platform. You can release funds through milestones.

Q: Can I cancel a project?
A: Yes, a project can be canceled anytime. Refunds depend on completed work and milestones.

Q: How is freelancer rating calculated?
A: Ratings are based on client reviews, completed projects, and overall performance. AverageRating is updated after each project review.

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click the Sign Up button on the homepage and fill in your name, email, and password. After verifying your email, your account will be activated.

Q: Can I use the platform without creating an account?

A: You can browse freelancers without an account, but you must create an account to post projects, hire freelancers, or contact them.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Click the “Forgot Password” option on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: Can I have both client and freelancer accounts?

A: Yes. You can hire freelancers as a client and also offer services as a freelancer using the same account.

## Hiring Freelancers
Q: How do I hire a freelancer?

A: Search for freelancers using filters like category, rating, and location. Open the freelancer profile and click the “Hire” or “Book” button to start the project.

Q: Can I contact freelancers before hiring?

A: Yes, you can message freelancers through the platform before confirming the project.

Q: How do I know if a freelancer is trustworthy?

A: Check their profile, ratings, reviews from previous clients, and completed projects.

Q: Can I hire multiple freelancers for one project?

A: Yes, you can hire multiple freelancers if your project requires different skills.
